About ATP
ATP was created by two programmers eager to start their own startup business in their spare time. They started writing their own applications and soon joined together; creating the company Pacworx and delivering ATP as their first mobile app. ATP is an open-source project. If you're interested in how it is built or want to contribute, check out our GitHub repo here: github.com/packowitz/atp-app
