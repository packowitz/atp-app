Thank you for trying ATP

ATP stands for "Ask The People" and is a platform where people can ask other people for their opinion. ATP offers the easiest and simplest way to do so. Every question contains two pictures and asks which the user prefers.

Ask a question

Choose two pictures from your gallery or your camera, select your target group and how many answers you want. Asking a question costs some pax (the in-app currency).

Answering questions

Hit the big blue button on the main screen to start answering questions. Select which picture you prefer and tap on that. If you are unsure hit "I don't know". With every answer you earn some pax that you can spend on starting your own questions.
